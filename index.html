Chay Newman

Artist's Biography

Chay Newman lives and works in Melbourne. She is currently studying creative writing at RMIT. In 2022-2023, she published work in RMIT’s student magazine ‘Catalyst’, AFIRC's 'Best Australian Films You've Never Seen', and a short story within an indie-published anthology book titled ‘Hurt and Comfort’. Inspired by the likes of Caitlyn Siehl and Emily Dickenson, Chay focuses on epic poetry and long-form prosaic stories.
